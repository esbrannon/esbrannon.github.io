Health Infobang

A blog about public health, medicine, and data science. Originally inspired by the word 'noraebang' (노래방 or singing room); a direct translation might be 'health information room'. An infobong (a homophone of 'infobang') is also defined as a 'laptop or similar internet-enabled device left out around the home for general use by guests'.
